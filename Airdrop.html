Airdrop, IDO & Launchpad

Airdrop — free token distribution to active project users. Often conducted before or after IDO.

IDO (Initial DEX Offering) — token sale through a decentralized exchange. Takes place on launchpad platforms like Polkastarter, DAO Maker, Seedify, etc.
